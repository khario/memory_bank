About

The startup iDoneThis once found that 41% of to-do list items are never completed. Wouldn't it be great if there was a to-do list application that helped you to remember the items on your lists, so that you actually made some headway with getting things done?

Enter memoryBankâ€“a to-do list application that actually works.

memoryBank has been design with simplicity in mind, so that you can focus on doing instead of just making lists. You can have as many lists as you need, with as many items as you want.

All items on your lists have due dates, to help you prioritise your workload. Regularly recurring items can be saved to a virtual bank, to help you remember to include them in future iterations.

You can create simple, text-only lists, or include more complex media with our new enhanced list feature.

memoryBank is a student project by Stewart Brown, Niall Morris, Khari Outram, and Calum Thain.
